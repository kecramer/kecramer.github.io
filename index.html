Projects

Quake Map

A simple webpage written with Javascript and jQuery. Leveraging the Google Maps API and USGS geojson data to list earthquakes occuring over the past week or month, and plotting them on a simple map.

This project, completed as part of General Assembly's Web Development Immersive class, was meant to excercise our ability to work with third party data and AJAX requests.

Head of Quality

Spent two and a half years building out Imgur's first QA team. Created processes for and oversaw the regular completion of manually testing new products in Imgur's iOS, Android, and Web properties throughout all stages of development.
